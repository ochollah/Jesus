The Prayer of Salvation

Please read this prayer slowly from your heart:

Lord Jesus, I come to You today. I acknowledge that I am a sinner in need of Your grace. I believe that You died on the cross for my sins and rose again on the third day to give me eternal life. I repent of my sins and turn away from my old ways.

Right now, I open the door of my heart and invite You to come in. Be my Lord, my Savior, and my Best Friend. Wash me clean with Your precious blood. Fill me with Your Holy Spirit and lead me in the way of righteousness.

I am now a child of God. Old things have passed away, and all things have become new. Thank You, Jesus, for saving me! Amen.
